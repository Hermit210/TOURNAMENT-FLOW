How It Works

        

          

            
1

            
Quest Completion

            
Player completes a quest in your game, triggering an on-chain event via the QuestController contract.

          

          

            
2

            
Kwala Workflow

            
Kwala detects the event and executes the serverless workflow, determining rewards and rarity.

          

          

            
3

            
NFT Minted

            
The workflow mints the NFT reward directly to the player's wallet and logs the transaction.
